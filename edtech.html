Economics Videos That Don't Suck (EVTDS)

A full-stack Learning Management System (LMS) designed to optimize student mastery of AP and University-level Economics.

This platform hosts a complete curriculum of instructional videos integrated with a proprietary review algorithm. The system tracks individual user performance to generate review quizzes tailored to their specific conceptual weaknesses.

  • Adaptive Learning Path: Automatically prioritizes quiz questions based on previous performance data.
  • Progress Tracking: Monitors video completion rates and quiz score trends in real-time.
  • Custom Pedagogy: Bridges the gap between passive video watching and active formative assessment.
